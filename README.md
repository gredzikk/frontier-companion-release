# Frontier Companion (v0.11.17-rc)

**Frontier Companion** is the ultimate fan-made utility for *Lightyear Frontier*. Designed to streamline your interstellar farming experience, this app helps you manage resources, calculate energy needs, and track milestones as you settle on the Frontier.

This release follows the **Automation Update** in-game and aims to provide all the data you need to build the perfect base.

-----

## 🚀 Features

  * **Item Encyclopedia & Crafting:** View details for every item and their crafting recipes.
  * **Item Basket:** Add multiple items to your list to calculate the total raw resource cost for your projects.
  * **Energy Balance Calculator:** Plan your base's power grid by adding constructs and matching them with the necessary generators.
  * **Interactive World Map:** Locate ore nodes, wild plant spawn points, and critter habitats.
  * **Farming & Ranching:** Detailed stats on soil types, plant growth, and hatchable critter stats (with screenshots\!).
  * **Mech Customization:** Planning tools for mech chips, body parts, and loadouts.
  * **Logistics & Storage:** Reference guides for fuel types, storage capacities, and merchant bundles.
  * **Progress Tracking:** Track delivery cannon milestones and base upgrade requirements.

-----

## 📥 Installation (Android)

As of the current Phase 1 release, we are providing direct APK downloads.

1.  **Check Architecture:** Determine if your device is `arm64-v8a` or `armeabi-v7a`.
2.  **Download:** Select the appropriate APK from the [Releases page](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://github.com/your-repo/releases).
3.  **Permissions:** You may need to enable **"Install from unknown sources"** in your Android settings.
4.  **Privacy:** The app is fully offline and requires zero invasive permissions.
